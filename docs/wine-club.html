Thank you for considering the Santori Wine Club

With each type of club, you will enjoy a regular shipment of our wine for your cellar and enjoyment. You can join our wine club to enjoy discounts on wine purchases, join us for special membership events and receive select wines periodically during the year.

As a wine club member, you will receive up to two shipments each year. These will generally be timed for Fall and Spring. Each shipment will contain between three to twelve bottles.
